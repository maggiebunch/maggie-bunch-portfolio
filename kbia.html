KBIA

Strategy and content for Columbia, Missouri’s NPR affiliate

Project type

Strategy and content

Date

2025

Location

Columbia, Missouri

As a social and audience strategist for KBIA, Columbia, Missouri’s NPR affiliate, I developed and presented long-term strategies to grow audience engagement across digital platforms. My work focused on translating KBIA’s audio-first reporting into visual, social-friendly formats that could connect with listeners where they already are.

I created diverse, platform-specific content including Instagram Reels, swipe-through educational carousels, and templates designed to highlight KBIA’s reporting in accessible, shareable ways. I also used tools like Parse.ly and Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to analyze audience behavior, guide SEO decisions, and refine content strategy based on real performance data.
